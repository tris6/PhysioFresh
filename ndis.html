Services

We are located in the newly renovated SPARC-Up Fitness gym in Brooklyn Park, a private facility that is free from the noise and traffic of a commercial gym. We have adaptive devices that transform ordinary gym equipment into a disability-friendly training space.

Hydrotherapy

We provide personalised one-on-one hydrotherapy sessions at the Minda Rehabilitation Pool in Brighton.

We offer a variety of delivery options depending on the client's capability and funding. Options range from weekly physio to 4-6 weekly oversight, with sessions delivered by an Allied Health Assistant or the client's support workers.

In-home visits

We provide in-home visits for clients who are unable to make it to our clinic.

Our services include physiotherapy assessment, manual therapy and home-based exercise prescribed for the client's capability, goals and condition.

Other services:

  • Transition care plan and support worker training
  • Comprehensive falls assessments
  • Physiotherapy reports

For Support Coordinators

We work closely with our support coordinators, providing regular updates on the client's progress, functional decline or change of circumstances to ensure you are up-to-date with their needs and any changes that occur.

We are aware that many of our clients do not want to access a commercial gym that is busy and noisy. We offer a unique training environment that is not accessible to the general public. Clients are also encouraged to train at home, or in their own gym, and are supported with an application that provides them with all their exercises and tracks their progress.

We would love to talk with you about the unique services we offer.

We work with clients who have a diagnosis of:

  • Cerebral palsy
  • Spinal cord injury
  • Down syndrome
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Previous stroke
  • Barnes syndrome
  • Autism
