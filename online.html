Physiotherapist-guided personal training

New to the gym, recovering from an injury, or finding yourself in a constant cycle of injuring while training - and not sure why? We all may believe strength training is a good idea in theory, but translating that into action can be challenging.

Simply knowing where to start can be overwhelming:

  • Feeling confident when you hit the gym to know what to do, and how to do it.
  • Choosing the right exercise for rehab and progressing that exercise as you improve.
  • Following an exercise program that gets you stronger without the constant set-back from injury.

These are some of the benefits that physiotherapist-guided personal training can provide.
