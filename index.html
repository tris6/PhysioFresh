"We empower individuals to achieve musculo-skeletal health. Our evidence-based, personalised care not only alleviates pain and improves function, but also educates and equips our patients with the tools they need to manage their conditions independently."

Walk in with pain, walk out with a plan.

PhysioFresh Solutions specialises in Mechanical Diagnosis and Therapy (MDT), an evidence-based approach developed by Robert McKenzie. The method emphasises patient empowerment through education, self-management, and active involvement in the rehab process.

Patients can experience significant reductions in pain and improved function in particular presentations. This allows them to return to daily activities quickly, even when symptoms are chronic (ie. greater than 3 months).
